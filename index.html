About Our School

Welcome to Iblue Primary School

Founded in 1995, Iblue Primary School has been providing quality education to children for over 25 years. We believe in nurturing young minds to become responsible, creative, and innovative thinkers.

Our mission is to provide a stimulating learning environment that empowers students to achieve their full potential academically, socially, and emotionally.

Over 25 years of educational excellence
800+ students enrolled currently
50+ qualified teaching staff
Modern facilities and infrastructure

Our Academics Program

Curriculum

We follow the national curriculum with enhancements in STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Mathematics) and creative arts.

  • English Language
  • Mathematics
  • Science
  • Social Studies
  • Creative Arts

Class Levels

We offer comprehensive education from Primary 1 to Primary 7, preparing students for secondary education.

  • Primary 1 - 2 (Ages 6-8)
  • Primary 3 - 4 (Ages 9-10)
  • Primary 5 - 6 (Ages 11-12)

Our Facilities

Science Laboratory
